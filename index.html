The Persian New Year is a time when family and friends gather to celebrate the arrival of spring and its new beginnings. Our special event provides you with a taste of the Iranian culture and its delicious cuisine with a dinner of Kabob and rice, traditional pastries, other scrumptious treats, live traditional Persian Music, Symphonic music, performance by Nuisha Dance and UC Davis dance group, and live DJ after dinner.

We invite you to experience the rich and beautiful Persian culture through our various performances.
We look forward to seeing you!
