Radin Rahimzadeh
Vice President
Dorood bar shoma (hello), present and future ISCAO members! I am a 3rd year International Relations and Economics major with the future goal of attending law school. However, my life-long aspiration has been to spread the knowledge (and the fun) of Iranian culture! I hope to familiarize Iranian diaspora and non-Iranians alike with Iranian culture; I also desire to satiate the nostalgic appetites of Iranians away from home. ISCAO is a warm and welcoming family that extends beyond the boundaries of UC Davis or graduating UC Davis. We would be delighted if you took part in our events and the expansion of our family! Be omideh didar (hope to see you soon)!
